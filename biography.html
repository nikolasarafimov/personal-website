My journey in the world of programming

My name is Nikola Sarafimov, and I’m a fourth-year Computer Science & Software Engineering student at FCSE – Skopje. I live in Skopje, Macedonia, where I study, build projects, and continuously grow as a developer.

My long-term goal is to grow as a Full-Stack Web Developer, contributing both independently and as part of a team.

Outside the world of code, I enjoy video editing, sports, reading, and hanging out with friends. These hobbies keep me creative, balanced, and inspired.
